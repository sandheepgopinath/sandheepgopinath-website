Project Case Studies

A look at how I've helped solve complex challenges.

VERINT SYSTEMS

Request Anything

Problem: Employees often don't know who to contact for internal requests, leading to delays and frustration.

Solution: Developed a "one-stop-shop" platform using generative AI to intelligently understand and route any employee request to the correct person or department automatically.

Impact: Scaled the platform from 0 to over 1,200 monthly active users and 10,000+ monthly requests, significantly improving internal efficiency.

HRS GROUP

Re-booking & Recommendation Engine

Problem: Business travelers could be saving money on hotel bookings, and the company needed to ensure high-quality hotel options.

Solution: Launched an automated system to monitor hotel prices and re-book at lower rates. Developed a recommendation engine to detect and remove low-quality hotels from the platform.

Impact: Achieved a 12% increase in revenue, reduced customer complaints by 80%, and cut onboarding time by 99% by making the platform self-service.

DANFOSS

Digital Transformation

Problem: Factory floor managers lacked real-time data and insights to monitor production quality and machine performance effectively.

Solution: Led the development of a SCADA-powered reporting platform for 118 machines across 4 countries and a vision-based quality monitoring system.

Impact: Delivered a +17% increase in production efficiency and a +2.4% increase in annual productivity across global factories.
